FAQ

What exactly is pay-per-click marketing?

Pay Per Click (PPC) marketing is a kind of search marketing in which marketers purchase website visitors by paying a charge each time one of their display adverts is clicked. It provides a bid-based web platform for marketers to bid on ad placement in sponsored search results. These adverts display on search engines when a potential customer searches for their business/product category using a relevant term.

What is the significance of PPC for my brand?

PPC advertising will provide you with instant results in terms of website traffic and conversions for your business. It also provides the greatest visibility in ROI because to its bid placement platform, which allows you to effortlessly regulate advertising spending and adjust your plans on the go.

How soon after starting a PPC campaign should I expect to see an increase in sales?

If done correctly, PPC may generate leads and conversions for your business almost instantly. We can manage ad costs on the fly and tune them for the optimum ROI for your campaign using the bidding platform.

Should my company employ pay-per-click (PPC) advertising?

When done correctly, PPC marketing may result in immediate increases in website traffic and conversions. As a consequence, it should be an important element of your advertising plan, particularly if you want to see rapid returns from your marketing investments.

What role does a landing page play in a PPC campaign?

A landing page is the web page that appears when a user clicks on your sponsored ad. The landing page's content and call to action buttons make it a crucial component in a PPC campaign to increase lead generation and conversions. The landing page may be one of your current website pages (as long as the CTA is placed correctly) or it can be constructed specifically for a campaign. Our staff is skilled in creating optimal landing pages that increase conversion.
