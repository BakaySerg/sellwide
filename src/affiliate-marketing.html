What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing are affiliate sites that join up for an affiliate marketing program and get a reward when recommendations result in sales.

This performance-based compensation model gives affiliates additional options for monetizing their sites while providing merchants with a greater return on investment.

We can help you put this Pay for Performance Marketing strategy into action. FAQ

How do affiliate networks generate revenue?

Affiliate networks generate money by charging merchants, often known as advertising, fees. These fees are normally negotiated upon with each individual advertiser and are paid as a percentage of each transaction.

How do we keep track of a publisher's sales activity?

Each publisher has a unique publisher ID, that is used in tracking cookies to determine which publisher generated which sales. This behavior is then visible in the affiliate network dashboard.

Can we have an affiliate program in more than one country?

Affiliate programs may be marketed in any nation where your product is needed and your affiliate network exists. If your site is available in many languages, it is advisable to set up an affiliate network for each language when feasible so that publishers may drive their viewers to the most appropriate version.

Is it necessary for me to pay taxes?

If you are unsure whether or whether to disclose the revenues you get through us as a person or as a firm, you should speak with your accountant. we do not provide any tax-related advice, consultancy, or services.

How Do Affiliates Earn Money?

Affiliates generate revenues by generating a qualified lead for a sale via their promotional materials. Typically, affiliate websites will have a specific brand page for the merchant with whom they are collaborating, where they will provide content that connects to items.
