FAQ

What is the purpose of email marketing?

Email marketing is used to communicate critical information, generate sales, strengthen brand loyalty, and create a community around your business (e.g. with a newsletter). Instead of a one-size-fits-all strategy, current email marketing focuses on personalization, segmentation, and permission.

How can I determine whether email marketing is a good fit for my company?

Email marketing should be part of your overall digital strategy if you currently have a contact database with clean email addresses and/or a plan for obtaining new email addresses AND you have something significant to say to those individuals.

Will an email marketing strategy increase my sales?

Email marketing may definitely increase sales if you encourage and inspire your audience to spend their money in your brand/products.

What is the best way to perform email marketing?

Email marketing should be approached with caution and attention. You and your marketing partners must figure out precisely what you will say and how you will say it.

How can I boost my subscriber base?

When it comes to gaining subscribers for your email marketing campaign, you should concentrate on the following:

  1. Honesty
  2. Transparency
